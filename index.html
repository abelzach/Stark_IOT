STARK_IOT

What is STARK ?

STARK is a smart street light managing system. It operates with the help of both Arduino and bOLT Iot.

Our project

Street lights are a vital component for road safety at night. But they consume a lot of electical power for its operation considering the many street lights that we have. The truth is that we do not always need street lights but only when required. This where Stark module plays its role.

IMPLEMENTATION

A STARK module activates at night and each STARK module is equipped with an ultrasonic sensor which detects any vehicle or a pedestrian passing by and it activates the street lights in a optimised and energy efficient way. STARK module also tracks the traffic density at night. This data will be send as a text message to the controller.
